Dear Sir/Ma'am,

We're excited to announce that the Opportunity Open Source Conference (OOSC) 4.0 will take place from August 28th to 30th, 2026, at the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Allahabad, India. As one of the largest student-led open-source conferences in the country, OOSC brings together a vibrant mix of developers, researchers, contributors, and enthusiasts to collaborate, share, and learn.

We are currently inviting speakers from across the open-source, academic, developer, and tech communities to submit proposals for talks and workshops.

Whether you work on open-source infrastructure, AI/ML, developer tools, cloud computing, cybersecurity, hardware, or community building, we'd love to hear from you!

Event Details:

📅 Dates: August 28–30, 2026
📍 Venue: Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Allahabad, India
🌐 Event Website: https://oosc.iiita.ac.in/
